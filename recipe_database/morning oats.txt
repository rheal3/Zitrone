
Morning Oats
    
Ingredients: 
1/2 cup  oats
1/2 cup  oat milk
2 tbsp   peanut butter
1        banana
         cinnamon

Steps: 
Step 1:  Mash banana in bowl.
Step 2:  Stir in 1 tbsp peanut butter, oats, and a dash of cinnamon.
Step 3:  Put mixture in pot and add oat milk.
Step 4:  Cook to desired consistency.
Step 5:  Cut remainder of banana into pieces for topping.
Step 6:  Top with banana pieces and remainder of peanut butter.
